# 📊 Adventure Works Dashboards

This repository contains a set of interactive dashboards built for **Adventure Works**, designed to provide insights into **Sales**, **Shipping**, and **Sales Person performance**.  
The dashboards are structured into three main sections with KPIs, charts, and visual breakdowns to support decision-making.

---

## 🔹 1. Sales Overview  
The **Sales Dashboard** provides a high-level summary of business performance.

### KPIs:
- 💰 **Total Sales:** 123M  
- 📋 **Number of Orders:** 31K  
- 📈 **Sales Growth %:** 22%  
- 📦 **Total Quantity:** 275K  

### Visuals:
- 📊 Top 10 Products by Orders (Bar Chart)  
- 🥧 Total Sales by Category (Pie Chart) – Bikes dominate the share  
- 🌳 Sales Decomposition by Category and Subcategory (Decomposition Tree)  
- 📉 Sales Growth % by Year (Line Chart)  

👉 Helps identify best-selling products, growth trends, and category-level contributions.

---

## 🔹 2. Shipping Overview  
The **Shipping Dashboard** focuses on logistics and distribution efficiency.

### KPIs:
- 🚚 **Total Freight:** 3M  
- 🏷️ **Total Tax:** 10M  
- 💵 **Total Subtotal:** 110M  

### Visuals:
- 📊 No. Orders by Territory Group (Bar Chart)  
- 🥧 No. Orders by Ship Method (Pie Chart) – Truck Ground is dominant  
- 🌍 Total Quantity by Territory (Bar Chart)  
- 📉 Total Freight by Month (Line Chart)  

👉 Supports tracking shipping costs, territory-level distribution, and method performance.

---

## 🔹 3. Sales Person Overview  
The **Sales Person Dashboard** highlights individual performance.

### Visuals:
- 📊 Top 10 Sales Persons by Sales (Bar Chart)  
- 📋 Top 10 Sales Persons by Orders (Horizontal Bar Chart)  

👉 Useful for monitoring sales performance, recognizing top contributors, and analyzing order distribution among salespersons.

---

## 🚀 Key Features  
- Clean and intuitive dashboard design  
- KPI cards for quick insights  
- Multiple visualization types: Bar, Line, Pie, Decomposition Tree  
- Covers three perspectives: **Sales, Shipping, Sales Person**  

---

## 🎯 Use Cases  
This project is ideal for:  
- Business performance analysis  
- Sales and shipping monitoring  
- Sales team performance evaluation  
- Management decision support
